Lesson 0: Introduction

This course will introduce you to the design principles that inform product development at Duolingo. We'll cover topics such as design conventions, consistency, and hierarchy to help you create products that are both visually appealing and easy to use.

This course will also allow you to better understand the design feedback you may get in Product Review, and what you may need to do to address it. By the end of this course, you'll be well-equipped to create beautiful, intuitive features for our learners.
