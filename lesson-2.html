Lesson 2

Be consistent

Stick to UI elements, layouts, and styles used elsewhere in the app. Use design patterns that are already familiar to the user.

Consistency in design means that every time you encounter the same type of design problem, you use the same type of design solution. When design is consistent, it is regular and predictable. By sticking to existing patterns, you reduce cognitive load, as learners do not need to relearn what a particular icon, word, or style represents.
