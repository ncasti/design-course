Lesson 3: Make good use of visual hierarchy

Size


Here's how you can use size effectively to convey visual hierarchy:

1. Stick to just three sizes (small, medium, and large) in order to maintain clarity and hierarchy. This will help ensure that different elements are easily distinguishable from one another.
